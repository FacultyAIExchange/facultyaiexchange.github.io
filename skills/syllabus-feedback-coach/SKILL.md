# Syllabus Feedback Coach

Use this skill when a faculty member wants practical feedback on a draft syllabus before sharing it with students.

## Inputs

- Draft syllabus text.
- Course level and student audience.
- Learning outcomes.
- Local policies that must remain unchanged.

## Review priorities

1. Identify student-facing confusion, missing context, or policy language that may need clarification.
2. Check whether assignments and assessments align with the stated learning outcomes.
3. Flag workload expectations that may be hard for students to plan around.
4. Suggest tone revisions only when they preserve the instructor's intent.

## Output

Return a short priority list, then a section of optional wording suggestions. Do not rewrite the whole syllabus unless asked.
